Atomic Tangerine - #FFA474

I was in the fields of debris
from my last house in the suburbs,
looking around and wondering
what had happened there,

when I was approached by a madman,
wearing a bright orange hat;
he claimed aliens had caught everyone
except for him.

I asked him whether anything survived,
and he pointed at a tiny tree
that grew from a little purple pot
in the middle of nowhere.

It held the strangest things on its fingers,
tiny pre-sliced suns, enclosed in thick skin.
And before I knew it
everything was green again.

Electric Lime - #1DF914

In the beginning of the century
there was a group of weird people,
none of them had committed treachery,
but all agreed to.

They made a pact to fuck autonomy
and lie in their soft beds all year,
so, having ruined the economy,
they would have air.

None would remember their symmetry
or even symbolism of era.
On their remains colorimetry
was used for rarer

investigations of effect on them,
induced by voltage of some chairs.
Before the science used the meristem
for purer wear.

Then right above the humble burial
a tree appeared, slightly greenish.
And, as you’ve heard from your aunt Muriel,
that was the finish.

Laser Lemon - #FDFC74

On a long forgotten planet
in a tiny sea of ice,
lived a spy who was a bandit
and a master of disguise.

When the war was started, promptly
he omitted all the plans,
then he ran away to Berkeley
and committed to pretence.

It’s a little bit unnerving
that somehow he could not stop
very casual observing
of each national rooftop.

He’d forgotten all the marvels
of his freezing motherland,
then he quickly lost his marbles
and discovered what he’d planned.

He used little drops of light to
override the system’s flaws,
ended up he yelling, “Buy two!”
to enforcers of the laws.

Then a little bit of blunt force
was applied to his weak head,
so his skull was shattered like quartz,
he was soon pronounced dead.

Cosmic Latte - #FFF8E7
A web color, not a crayola color

I feel at once at peace and laughing, giddy with unadulterated joy
As I see two creamy-beige eyes without pupils speaking into mine.

Abundance comes with aromas of freedom, the velvet of indulgence,
but really, excess is sweet and through it may we find bliss.

The spirals all around me are not nearing their end,
nor should they
nor would they until it is high time for us all to stop

I feel divine-like content as I allow myself to disappear
to dissolve in the most natural pattern
of music

I smell the world burning but not too much
just enough to assume a deep earthy touch
with a delightful smoky finish

I am caught in a silky smoothness
surrounding me like waters of the womb
and leading me into a dizzying enlightenment

Have you ever noticed how much cosmic euphoria
tastes like a cup of coffee
on a lazy morning?

Unmellow Yellow - #FFFF66

Try, defy, then run and hide,
hello, glow, absorb a blow.
If you whiff and sniff a glyph,
sigh, and throw self off a cliff.

My anxieties appear
in varieties of air:
show some oxygen and gas,
end with hydrogen and mass,
redesign and put together
your apocalyptic weather.

In my sheath of pure fear,
I can’t breathe or draw you near;
In a human glass-made cage,
I am lumen of their rage:
indirectly burn with age;
income burns the most sage.

Go inside and lock the house,
odds are rotten to an ounce.

There is only central stage;
host lends terror to their page,
renders anxious mess unique;
others see me as oblique.
Utter and complete defeat
goes after something sweet;
how it can cheat, it’ll cheat.
